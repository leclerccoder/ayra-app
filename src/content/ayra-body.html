About Us

Imagine • Design • Build

Spaces crafted with precision, comfort, and lasting beauty.

Based in Kuala Lumpur, Ayra Design & Build is a full-service interior design-and-build studio transforming homes and workplaces across the Klang Valley. We blend architectural thinking with refined craftsmanship delivering spaces that feel modern, calm, and timeless. From the first sketch to final handover, every line, material, and detail is considered with purpose.

We believe great design starts with people. Your routines, aspirations, and sense of style shape our approach. With a trusted network of specialists and makers, we manage the process end-to-end clear communication, transparent costing, and on-time delivery so you enjoy a seamless experience and a space that truly works for you.
